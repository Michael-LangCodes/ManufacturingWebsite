About Us

Our Story

Steel Fabrication was founded in 2005 in the heart of the American Midwest, rooted in a region known for its strong work ethic, manufacturing heritage, and commitment to quality craftsmanship. What started as a small family-owned welding shop in Indiana has grown into a full-service steel fabrication company serving clients across the nation. Our founders, armed with decades of combined experience in structural steel and metal fabrication, built the company on principles of integrity, precision, and customer satisfaction.

Over the past two decades, we have expanded our capabilities while maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that defined our early work. From our Midwest headquarters, we have completed projects spanning coast to coast, earning a reputation for reliability, quality, and on-time delivery that reflects the values of the communities we call home.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional steel fabrication solutions that exceed our clients' expectations through superior craftsmanship, innovative techniques, and unwavering commitment to quality. We strive to be the most trusted partner for structural steel fabrication by combining Midwest values of hard work and honesty with cutting-edge technology and industry expertise.

Why Choose Us

Midwest Work Ethic: Our team embodies the traditional values of the Midwest—punctuality, reliability, and a handshake that means something. We believe in doing the job right the first time, every time.

Comprehensive Capabilities: From initial design consultation through final installation, we offer end-to-end steel fabrication services under one roof. Our state-of-the-art facility houses advanced laser cutting, CNC forming, robotic welding, and finishing capabilities.

Quality Assurance: Every project undergoes rigorous quality control inspections at each stage of production. We maintain certifications with the American Welding Society (AWS) and adhere to strict industry standards to ensure structural integrity and longevity.

Experienced Team: Our workforce includes certified welders, skilled fabricators, and experienced project managers with an average of 15+ years in the steel industry. This depth of expertise allows us to tackle projects of any complexity with confidence.

Our Commitment

We are committed to building lasting relationships with our clients through transparent communication, competitive pricing, and consistent delivery. Whether you need custom architectural steelwork, structural components for commercial construction, or precision fabrication for industrial applications, our team is dedicated to bringing your vision to life with the quality and professionalism you deserve.

From our Midwest roots to serving clients nationwide, Steel Fabrication remains committed to the values that have guided us since day one: quality craftsmanship, honest dealing, and a dedication to seeing our customers succeed.
