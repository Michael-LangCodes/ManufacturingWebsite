Solutions

At Steel Fabrication, we provide comprehensive steel fabrication solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of your projects. Our team of skilled professionals utilizes state-of-the-art equipment and proven techniques to deliver exceptional results across a wide range of services.

Welding

Our certified welders deliver precision welding services across multiple processes including MIG, TIG, Stick, and Flux-Cored Arc Welding (FCAW). We specialize in structural steel welding for buildings, bridges, and industrial facilities, ensuring every weld meets or exceeds AWS (American Welding Society) standards. Whether you need custom fabrication, repair work, or large-scale production welding, our team has the expertise to handle projects of any complexity. We work with carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and specialty alloys to provide solutions that meet your exact specifications.

Stamping

Steel stamping is a cost-effective manufacturing process for producing high volumes of precision metal parts. Our stamping services include blanking, piercing, bending, and forming operations using advanced press equipment. We can handle materials ranging from thin gauge sheet metal to heavy plate steel, producing everything from small brackets and connectors to large structural components. Our in-house tool and die capabilities ensure quick turnaround times and consistent quality across production runs. Stamping is ideal for applications requiring tight tolerances and repeatable accuracy.

Laser Cutting

Our fiber laser cutting technology delivers exceptional precision and speed for cutting steel plates, sheets, and structural members. Laser cutting offers superior edge quality, minimal material waste, and the ability to produce complex geometries that would be difficult or impossible with traditional cutting methods. We can process materials up to several inches thick with tolerances as tight as ±0.005 inches. This versatile process is perfect for creating custom parts, architectural features, machinery components, and intricate decorative elements. Our CNC-programmed systems ensure consistent results from the first piece to the thousandth.

Forming

Metal forming transforms flat steel stock into three-dimensional shapes through controlled deformation processes. Our forming capabilities include roll forming, stretch forming, deep drawing, and CNC press brake operations. We can create cylinders, cones, boxes, channels, and custom profiles to meet your design requirements. Our experienced operators work with various steel grades and thicknesses, from light-gauge sheet metal to heavy plate. Forming is essential for creating structural components, architectural elements, enclosures, and vessels used across construction, manufacturing, and industrial applications.

Bending

Precision bending is a fundamental steel fabrication process that shapes metal into specific angles and configurations. Using CNC press brakes and plate rolls, we achieve accurate bends with consistent radii and minimal springback. Our bending services handle everything from simple 90-degree bends to complex multi-bend geometries in a single setup. We work with structural beams, channels, angles, tubing, and plate materials to create frames, supports, brackets, and architectural features. Our team carefully calculates bend allowances and sequences to ensure dimensional accuracy and structural integrity in every finished component.
