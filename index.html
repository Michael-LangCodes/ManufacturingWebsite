Laser Cutting

Tight-tolerance capabilities for a wide range of thickness of steels. All the way from 3 Gauge down to 25 Gauge steel, we have the capabilites to fabricate your components.

Forming

Forming capabilities allow for the creation of 3D components to bring your project from paper to your hands. We have an experienced team that can lead the design through the Design For Manufacturing (DFM) process to ensure your parts come out to print.

Welding

Here at Steel Fabrication we also have experienced technicians and state-of-the-art machinery to weld components to meet your design specification.

Bending

Bending capabilities for all your project needs. Capabilities to bend a range of materials in various thicknesses.
